FAQ

Is this a replacement for pest control contractors?

No. Pest Intel supports monitoring/compliance and early detection. Professional intervention is still required when risk triggers are met.

Is there a contract?

Yes — 12-month minimum term with 30 days’ notice to end at minimum term completion.

What happens after month 3 pricing?

Your plan moves from intro pricing to standard monthly pricing for months 4–12.

What is included in setup?

Site banding, onboarding guidance, monitoring pack allocation, and inspection workflow activation.

What if we miss an inspection?

You receive reminders and can complete late; repeated misses increase risk and should be addressed by management.

What counts as a triage case?

One submitted issue for review (photos/notes) resulting in one triage decision and recommended action.
